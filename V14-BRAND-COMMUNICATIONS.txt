NETLIFY
The existing variables are sufficient:
BREVO_API_KEY
BREVO_LIST_ID

Optional existing sender overrides:
BREVO_SENDER_EMAIL
BREVO_SENDER_NAME

IMPORTANT
These automatic acknowledgements trigger when visitors use the website forms.
Messages sent directly to partners@, press@ or licensing@ are delivered to the STRATO mailbox but are not automatically answered by this website function. For direct-email autoresponders, configure a STRATO vacation/autoreply rule separately.

DEPLOY
