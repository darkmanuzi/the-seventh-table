THE SEVENTH TABLE — V16 DIRECT FORMS

What changed
- Guest, Partner, Press and Licensing forms call a Netlify Function directly.
- No Netlify HTTP POST notification is required.
- Branded Brevo emails and internal business messages are handled by forms-api.
- Successful submissions are additionally archived in Netlify Forms as a best-effort step.
- Visible error messages are shown if the API fails; users are no longer redirected on silent failure.

Required Netlify environment variables
- BREVO_API_KEY
- BREVO_LIST_ID

Optional
- BREVO_PARTNERS_LIST_ID=5
- BREVO_SENDER_EMAIL=hello@the-seventh-table.com
- BREVO_SENDER_NAME=The Seventh Table

After deployment
1. Remove the old HTTP POST notification under Netlify form notifications. It is no longer used.
2. Keep or remove the simple Netlify email notification according to preference. It is independent of the branded Brevo emails.
3. Test Guest and Partner once.
4. Inspect Logs & metrics → Functions → forms-api if needed.
