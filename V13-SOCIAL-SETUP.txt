THE SEVENTH TABLE — V13 SOCIAL LAUNCH EDITION

WHAT IS AUTOMATIC NOW
- Daily Netlify scheduled function prepares one elegant evergreen post.
- The post is sent to a webhook at 10:00 UTC each day.
- UTM tracking is included in every campaign link.
- Visitors can share the website through native sharing, X, Facebook and LinkedIn.
- An RSS feed is available at /feed.xml.

IMPORTANT
The website cannot publish directly to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook or LinkedIn without each platform's permission/API connection. The safe and practical route is:
Netlify scheduled function -> Make/Zapier/Buffer/Metricool webhook -> connected social accounts.

NETLIFY ENVIRONMENT VARIABLE
SOCIAL_WEBHOOK_URL = webhook URL from Make, Zapier, Buffer or another approved scheduler.
Optional:
SOCIAL_WEBHOOK_SECRET = your own random secret, if the receiving workflow checks it.

DEPLOY
1. Extract this folder.
2. Open Terminal in the folder.
3. Run: netlify deploy --prod

NO SOCIAL WEBHOOK YET?
No problem. The website and Guest List continue working normally. The scheduled function simply logs “Not configured” until SOCIAL_WEBHOOK_URL is added.

CONTENT
The seven rotating posts are stored in social-campaigns.json and mirrored in netlify/functions/social-campaign.mjs.
Edit the wording there before deploying when you want a new weekly campaign.
