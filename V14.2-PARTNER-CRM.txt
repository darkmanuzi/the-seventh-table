THE SEVENTH TABLE — V14.2 PARTNER CRM

Changes:
- Partner form entries are automatically added to Brevo list #5: The Seventh Table – Partners.
- Partner contacts remain separate from the Guest List (#4).
- The submitting company receives a personalised branded acknowledgement.
- The internal enquiry still arrives at partners@the-seventh-table.com.
- Reply-To points to the submitting partner.

Optional Netlify environment variable:
BREVO_PARTNERS_LIST_ID=5

The code defaults to list 5, so the variable is optional.

Deploy from this exact folder:
netlify deploy --prod
