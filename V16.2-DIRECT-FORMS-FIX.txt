# V16.2 — Direct Forms Listener Fix

This patch replaces `forms-direct.js` with a delegated submit listener.

Why:
- the previous script attached listeners only to forms present at script load time;
- if a form was not yet present, the browser submitted it natively to Netlify;
- that produced only the short Netlify data email.

After this patch:
- Guest List shows `Reserving your seat…`
- Business forms show `Sending your request…`
- the browser calls `/.netlify/functions/forms-api`
- Brevo handles the branded confirmation mail
